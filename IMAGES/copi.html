Dine & Drink

Polo Beach Club: Where Coastal Luxury Meets Mediterranean Flavors

A Culinary Escape with Mediterranean Flavors and Local Inspirations

At Polo Beach Club, we create an unparalleled beachfront dining experience, combining the richness of Mediterranean-inspired cuisine with vibrant Ghanaian flavors. Nestled along Labadi Beach, our menu celebrates fresh, locally-sourced ingredients, offering an exquisite range of delicacies designed to excite every palate.

Our chefs meticulously prepare each dish, blending traditional cooking techniques with a creative twist, ensuring every bite reflects authenticity and bold flavors. From our signature Aioli Octopus infused with garlic olive oil to our indulgent Spicy Goat and the vibrant Jollof Paella filled with prawns, squid, and black olives, Polo Beach Club offers a taste journey for every visitor.
